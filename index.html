Desná Stroller ❌ ☀️ Best in sun 🌧️ Muddy when wet

Forest trail along Bílá Desná to the historical dam ruins. Educational panels, wooden bridges, water features. Short segments perfect for stopping.

5yo walks most of it. 2yo in carrier for steep parts. Best adventure-to-effort ratio — built-in "wow" moments keep them going.

Jizerka village Stroller ⚠️ ☀️ Calm day 💨 Exposed

Open peat bog with wooden boardwalks. Safírový potok — rocks and water for kids. Wide-open mountain views.

Kids Guide

Zero-stress terrain. High chance of water play near the stream. Very windy — always bring extra layers.
